# Unlettrd

Philosophy and Poetry for the Present Moment.

A modern literary magazine examining current and historical events through the lens of accessible philosophy, distilling complex truths into poetry that resonates.

Unlettrd publishes weekly articles paired with original poetry across four categories:

- **The Moment**: Current events from the past 1-4 weeks
- **The Mirror**: Historical events with modern relevance
- **The Current**: Ongoing cultural phenomena and trends
- **The Stand**: Direct philosophical positions and editorials
